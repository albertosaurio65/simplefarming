Simple Farming is a Minecraft 1.14+ agriculture mod

Features
  • New crops
  • Berry bushes
  • Fruit trees
  • New meals
  • Alcohol brewing
Complementary Mods
  • Just Enough Items (JEI)
  • AppleSkin
  • Right Click, Get Crops
Resource Packs
  • BeautyCrops
Feedback

Any issues should be submitted on the GitHub issue tracker. If you would like to suggest a new feature, feel free to join the community Discord.

Disclaimer

CurseForge is the only website where builds are uploaded. Be cautious when downloading mods from other sites.
